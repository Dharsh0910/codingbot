Interesting facts about cricket

1. The first recorded international cricket match was between the United States and Canada in 1844.
2. The longest cricket match lasted 12 days, played between England and South Africa in 1939.
3. The first Cricket World Cup was held in 1975, with the West Indies emerging as champions.
4. Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most runs and centuries in both Test and ODI cricket.
5. Sir Donald Bradman has the highest Test batting average of 99.94.
6. Cricket was an Olympic sport in 1900, with Great Britain winning the gold medal.
7. The Ashes, a famous series between England and Australia, began in 1882.
8. Twenty20 (T20) cricket, introduced in 2003, has boosted the sport's popularity.
9. Cricket is the second most popular sport globally, with a huge following in South Asia.
10. There are ten ways a batsman can be dismissed, including bowled, caught, and run out.
