12th and 10th Results

NEW DELHI: The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Government Examinations (TNDGE) has released the TN 10th Result 2024 today, May 10. Students who have participated in the TN SSLC 10th Examinations 2024 can download their results from the official website at tnresults.nic.in. CGBSE Class 10 and 12 results are out The overall pass percentage of Class 10 is 75.64% The overall pass percentage of Class 12 is 87.04% The Class 12 board examination was conducted from March 1st to March 23rd, 2024, across various exam centres in the state, and the Class 10 exams were held from March 2nd to March 21st, 2024, from 9:15 am to 12:15 pm.
